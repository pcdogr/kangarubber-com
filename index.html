Who We Are

We cater to a diverse range of industry clients, including interior and exterior designers, home decor projects, various venues such as hotels, malls, and resorts. Our clients also include furniture importers and industrial product packaging industries.

With our extensive experience, rapid response, and reasonable pricing, we proudly manufacture most of our products in Australia while maintaining strong relationships with overseas industry partners.

Our services are not limited to soft furnishings, fabric processing, foam products, and various rubber items. We offer specialized cutting services to meet large-scale production demands, reduce procurement costs, or fill gaps in the local market supply.

Our Core Services

Foam

Custom foam cutting, shaping and fabrication for furniture, seating, packaging and industrial applications. Wide range of densities and specifications available.

Cushion

Custom cushion manufacturing for indoor and outdoor furniture, marine, caravan, and window seat applications. Fabric wrapping and upholstery services available.

Cutting

Specialized cutting services for large-scale production demands. Reduce procurement costs and fill gaps in local market supply with our precision cutting capabilities.

Rubber

Custom rubber products including gaskets, seals, washers, and industrial components. Various rubber grades and formulations to suit your specific requirements.
