About Us

Manufacturing the Difference

We cater to interior and exterior designers, home decor projects, hotels, malls, resorts, furniture importers and industrial packaging industries. Core Services

What We Do

From custom foam cutting to full cushion manufacturing — we deliver quality across every project.

Foam

Custom cutting, shaping and fabrication for furniture, seating, packaging and industrial use. Wide range of densities.

Cushion

Indoor and outdoor cushions, marine, caravan and window seat applications. Fabric wrapping and upholstery available.

Cutting

Specialized large-scale cutting services to reduce procurement costs and fill local supply gaps with precision capabilities.

Rubber

Custom gaskets, seals, washers and industrial rubber components. Various grades and formulations to suit your requirements.
